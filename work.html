spoon app - interaction design si 582

january 2024 - april 2024

this course was centered around desiging an artifact to support a local community. due to michigan's constant unsupport for their disabled community members, i created an app that allows users to naviagte campus in an easier and safer way.

features of this app include:

  1. calendar syncing to allow automatic reminders for departure times
  2. ride ordering, with multiple options to fit varrying mobility aids
  3. real time maps with hazard reporting
  4. user profile to privately input assistance preferences to provide the user a sense of autonomy
  5. and more!

whispr - analytical product design desci 501

august 2023 - december 2023

working with a team, we created a wearable device that dampens a user's voice up to 20db. it utilizied active and passive noise cancelling technologies, in combination with live audio monitoring and playback, to give a user maximum sound attenuation while practing singing, talking, or taking private phonecalls. this project was born from michigan's desci 501 - analytical product design course, where we learned the phases of traditional design processes, starting from customer discovey and ending with creating a full fledge business plan. see our full portfolio for this project by visiting this link.
